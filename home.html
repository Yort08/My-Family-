About The Dancel Family

The Dancel family is rooted in the Philippines, with strong values of education, innovation, and family unity. Led by patriarch Rolando D. Dancel and matriarch Juvilyn D. Dancel, our family comprises talented individuals spanning various professions—from healthcare and technology to civil engineering and education.

This digital archive was created to preserve our family's story for generations to come. It serves as a testament to our shared heritage and celebrates the achievements and connections that bind us together.
